## 🎯 Priority Rules

### Task Prioritization

When multiple tasks are in `/Needs_Action`, prioritize in this order:

1. **P0 - Critical:** Emergency/Urgent client requests
2. **P1 - High:** Revenue-generating activities (invoices, proposals)
3. **P2 - Normal:** Regular communications and tasks
4. **P3 - Low:** Administrative and optimization tasks
